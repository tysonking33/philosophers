Mutex:
	-	locking mechanism to synchronize acess to a resource
	-	allows for mutal exclusion (either producer or consumer can have the key and do work, the other has to wait)
	-	1 thread per entire buffer

Semaphore:
	-	signaling mehnism
	-	gernatlize mutex
	-	Mutex's single bufffer 1 4kb buffer = Semaphore's multiple buffers 4 1kb buffers
	-	Consumer and producer accan work on differnt buffers simultaneously

Can a locked mutex be locked twice?
	-	No, the mutex must be unlocked before locking again

What happens if a non-recursive mutex is locked more that once?
	-	Deadlock
